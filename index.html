Getting Started

How to Use StudyForge AI

Generate your first study session in under 60 seconds.

  1. 1

    Get Your Free Gemini API Key

    Visit Google AI Studio, sign in with a Google account, and generate a free API key. The Gemini free tier provides up to 1,000 AI requests per day — more than enough for daily studying.

  2. 2

    Enter Your API Key

    Click the "API Key" button in the top header. Paste your Gemini API key and click Save. Your key is stored securely in your browser's local storage and never shared.

  3. 3

    Upload a Study PDF

    Drag and drop any PDF document — lecture slides, textbook chapters, revision notes — onto the upload zone. Files up to 15MB are supported. Your file is read locally and never uploaded.

  4. 4

    Choose a Study Mode

    Select from Summary, Quiz, Flashcards, or Exam Prep mode. For Quiz mode, pick the number of questions (10, 20, or 30). Each mode generates content specifically tailored to your uploaded document.

  5. 5

    Generate & Study

    Click "Generate Session" and let Gemini AI process your document. In seconds, you'll have interactive study materials personalized to your exact content. Sessions are saved automatically for future use.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about StudyForge AI.

Can I generate study flashcards and quizzes from a PDF completely free?
Yes! StudyForge AI is completely free to use. By supplying your own Gemini API key, you access Google's AI infrastructure directly without any subscription costs. The Gemini API free tier allows up to 1,000 requests per day and 15 requests per minute — more than enough for daily studying.
Does StudyForge AI support active recall and spaced repetition?
Absolutely. The Quiz and Flashcard modes are built specifically around active recall principles — the scientifically proven method for improving memory retention. The 3D flip flashcards and instant-feedback quiz system help you test yourself on material rather than passively re-reading, which research shows dramatically improves long-term recall.
Is my PDF data safe? Are my files uploaded anywhere?
Your PDF files are 100% private. All file processing happens locally in your browser using PDF.js — no file data is ever sent to any server. Only the plain extracted text from the PDF is sent to Google's official Gemini API to generate study materials. Your API key is stored only in your browser's local storage, never on external databases.
What is the maximum PDF file size I can upload?
You can upload study PDFs up to 15MB in size. For very long documents, StudyForge AI automatically trims the extracted text to fit within the Gemini API context window while preserving the most relevant content. This ensures fast, accurate generation even for long textbook chapters.
How do I get a free Google Gemini API key?
Getting a free Gemini API key takes under 2 minutes: (1) Go to Google AI Studio. (2) Log in with any Google account. (3) Click "Get API Key" in the top left. (4) Click "Create API Key", select a project, and copy your key. Paste it into StudyForge AI's API Key field. It's completely free for students and developers.
What kinds of PDFs work best with StudyForge AI?
StudyForge AI works best with text-based PDFs such as lecture slides, textbook chapters, academic papers, revision notes, and course handouts. PDFs that consist primarily of scanned images or handwritten notes may not extract text well, as the tool uses PDF.js for client-side text extraction rather than OCR.
Can I access my previous study sessions?
Yes. All generated study sessions — quizzes, flashcards, summaries, and exam prep sets — are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Click the "Sessions" button in the header to open the history drawer and reload any past session instantly.
